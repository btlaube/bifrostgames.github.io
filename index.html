About Me

Hi there!

My name is Ben Laube, and I'm the independent game developer behind BifrostGames. I've been creating games since 2019, developing projects through my university's Game Development Club, and participating in game jams like GMTK.

Through BifrostGames, I explore new mechanics, experiment with ideas, and continue growing as both a developer and a creator. My goal is to craft games that are not only fun to play but memorable long after the credits roll.
