Eternal Blade

Eternal Blade is an infinite, procedurally generated dungeon crawler where you play as a sword that can sacrifice its wielder to save itself.

Shadow Retriever

Shadow Retriever puts you in the role of the alchemist's shadow, summoned to retrieve powerful lost artifacts. Explore the alchemist's lair and unlock new alchemical abilities from the items you collect to aid in your quest.

Shifty Scales

Shifty Scales is a puzzle game where you run a shop that fixes mis-scaled items with a special scale-shifting machine. Solve tricky puzzles and satisfy customer orders by resizing and reassembling objects.

Deep Space Clean Up

Deep Space Clean Up puts you in the role of a space janitor sent to clean up the lost ISS Phoenix. After crash landing, explore the abandoned station, collect DNA samples, and use syringes to unlock new abilities as you uncover its dark secrets.

At Large

At Large is a stealth-based survival game where you play as an escaped convict trying to blend into a crowd every 5 seconds to avoid detection.

Vice's Grip

Vice's Grip is a fast-paced escape game where you're trapped by the Trash Compactor Killer. Can you escape the deadly compactor before it's too late?
