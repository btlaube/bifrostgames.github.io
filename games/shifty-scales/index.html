Shifty Scales

Premise:

Townspeople have been reporting a strange phenomenon; their belongings have mysteriously resized! Big things are now too small, and small things are too large. Luckily, youâ€™re the owner of Shifty Scales, the only shop in town capable of fixing these peculiar problems with your scale-shifting machine. Swap item sizes, solve tricky puzzles, and reassemble objects to solve customer orders.

How To Play:

  • Receive orders of incorrectly scaled items and parts.
  • Drag a part into both sides of the scale machine, then either:
    • Swap their scales,
    • Match both to the smaller,
    • or match both to the larger. 
  • Reassemble the rescaled parts into the complete shape(s).
  • Send the order for review.

Controls:

  • Mouse Controls: Drag and drop items into the machine.
  • Machine Activation: Click button on machine to initiate scale swap or match.
    • Left Button: Match both part scales to the smaller scale.
    • Right Button: Match both part scales to the larger scale.
    • Middle Button: Swap part scales.
  • Submit Order: Click "Submit Order" once all items are correctly resized.

Key Features

  • Unique Puzzle Mechanic: Swap the scales of various objects and figure out the correct size combinations.
  • Drag-and-Drop Gameplay: Drag and place items into the machine, then reassemble them into their proper forms.
  • Customer Orders: Receive incorrectly scales items from customers and swap or match scales to satisfy the customer's order.
